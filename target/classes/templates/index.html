Thailand defeat USA 69-22 in Kabaddi World Cup
short by Rajdeep Puri / 02:07 am on 17 Oct 2016,Monday
Thailand defeated USA 69-22 in their Group B encounter of the Kabaddi World Cup in Ahmedabad on Sunday. Thai skipper Khomsan Thongkham scored 14 points, while USA managed three points in the first half. Thailand are now second in Group B with 15 points from four matches, while USA remain winless after four games and are last in the group.
